Conscious Café Project

Speak now, to end food waste

According to the NRA (National Restaurant Association), most restraunts waste between 4%-10% of their purchased food before it even reaches their customers. This is mainly due to overordering and inaccurate demand estimations. Through the Conscious Café, we hope to minimize the national food waste by enabling restaurant owners to more accurately order supply.

Step 1:

Gather your restaurant data (current quantities of food ordered by ingredient, average weekly customers, weekly food waste, dish ordering statistics, ingredients in each dish)

Step 3:

Let our algorithm do the work for you. Using our predictive algorithm, it will estimate the demand of different dishes, encouraging or discouraging ingredient orders in order to minimize waste.

Step 2:

Input this data into the calculator.

Step 4:

Adjust your restaurant ingredient orders for a more environmentally friendly, waste-free operation!
